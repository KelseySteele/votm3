Overview

Vazzt Service enables you, while on the move in a vehicle, in an aircraft, or on foot with a camera connected to a computer/laptop/netbook to send live video streams and video files to your own website for your viewers to access.

Vazzt Service is a complete end-to-end solution. Vazzt provides the video encoding, network super pipe, and delivery capability.

Instructions

The purpose of this website is to help you use our VOTM product.

Check out our simple step-by-step list of instructions for how to use Video-On-The Move. After exploring these steps, you will be able to prepare and set-up VOTM and then broadcast your own live video.
